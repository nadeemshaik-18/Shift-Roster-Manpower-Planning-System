# 📊 Shift Roster & Manpower Planning System

A structured **Shift Roster & Workforce Planning solution** designed for **E-Commerce Operations** to efficiently manage employee scheduling, ensure optimal headcount coverage, and maintain operational continuity.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project provides a **systematic approach to workforce planning**, including:

- Weekly shift roster creation  
- Headcount validation across shifts  
- Leave & week-off management  
- Shift swap tracking  
- Operational compliance and reporting  

It is ideal for teams handling:
- Customer Support  
- Warehouse Operations  
- Delivery Operations  

---

## 🎯 Objective

To ensure:
- ✅ Minimum staffing requirements are always met  
- ✅ Fair distribution of shifts and week-offs  
- ✅ Structured handling of leaves and swaps  
- ✅ Zero operational disruptions due to manpower gaps  

---

## 🧩 Key Features

### 📅 Weekly Shift Roster Planning
- Assign shifts for all employees across 7 days
- Supports:
  - Morning (M)
  - Afternoon (A)
  - Night (N)
  - Week-Off (W)
  - Leave (L)

---

### 📊 Automated Headcount Validation
- Built-in summary checks
- Identifies:
  - ✅ Proper coverage (OK)
  - ❌ Shortages (SHORTAGE)

---

### 🔄 Shift Swap Management
- Tracks swap requests
- Ensures no impact on shift coverage
- Maintains approval logs

---

### 📝 Leave & Week-Off Tracking
- Pre-planned week-offs (1 per employee/week)
- Approved leave integration
- Avoids scheduling conflicts

---

### 📈 Weekly Performance Review
- Compare planned vs actual attendance
- Identify:
  - Unplanned absences
  - High swap frequency
  - Staffing gaps

## ⏰ Shift Structure

| Code | Shift       | Timing             | Min Headcount |
|------|------------|-------------------|--------------|
| M    | Morning     | 6:00 AM – 2:00 PM  | 8 Employees  |
| A    | Afternoon   | 2:00 PM – 10:00 PM | 8 Employees  |
| N    | Night       | 10:00 PM – 6:00 AM | 6 Employees  |
| W    | Week-Off    | —                 | Max 6/day    |
| L    | Leave       | —                 | As approved  |

---

## ⚙️ Process Workflow

1. Receive Headcount Plan  
2. Check Leave & Week-Off Requests  
3. Build Weekly Roster  
4. Validate Headcount Coverage  
5. Handle Shift Swap Requests  
6. Publish Final Roster  
7. Weekly Review & Reporting  

---

## 📏 Rules & Compliance

- Every employee must have **1 week-off per week**
- Maintain **minimum headcount at all times**
- No more than **3 consecutive night shifts**
- All swaps must be **logged and approved**
- Roster must be published by **Sunday 6 PM**

---

## 🚨 Escalation Matrix

| Scenario                          | Action                          | Escalation To     |
|----------------------------------|----------------------------------|------------------|
| Shift shortage                   | Reassign / Overtime              | Team Lead        |
| Excess swaps (>3/week)           | Review & approval                | Ops Manager      |
| Unplanned absence                | Mark & follow-up                 | HR & Team Lead   |
| Night shift shortage (2+ days)   | Emergency action                 | Workforce Planner|
